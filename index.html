Welcome to Green Light Creation Performaning Art

Green Light Performance Art is a dynamic training program dedicated to nurturing the creative potential of children aged 4 to 16 years. Our program offers a diverse range of classes, including Art and Crafts, Dance, Tie-Dye, and Computer Skills, all designed to inspire and empower young minds.
