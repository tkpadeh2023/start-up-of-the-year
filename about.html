About

Mission Statement: At Green Light Performance Art, our mission is to ignite the creative spark in every child by providing a nurturing environment where they can explore and develop their talents in art, dance, tie-dye, and computer skills. We believe in fostering creativity, confidence, and a love for learning in children aged 4 to 16 years, helping them shine brightly in all aspects of life.
