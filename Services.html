Programs

  1. Art and Crafts:
    • Explore various mediums such as painting, drawing, sculpture, and more.
    • Projects designed to enhance creativity and fine motor skills.
    • Suitable for all skill levels, with personalized guidance.
  2. Dance
    • Learn different dance styles, including ballet, hip-hop, and contemporary.
    • Focus on rhythm, coordination, and expression through movement.
    • Performance opportunities to showcase talent.
  3. Tie-Dye:
    • Hands-on workshops to create unique tie-dye designs.
    • Learn about color theory and design principles.
    • Fun, creative sessions that result in wearable art.
  4. Computer Skills:
    • Introduction to basic computer skills and digital literacy.
    • Learn to create digital art, simple animations, and beginner coding.
    • Safe and monitored online environment.
