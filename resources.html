Resources for Green Light Performance Art

  1. Creative Materials and Supplies:

    • Art Kits: Each child receives an art kit containing paints, brushes, sketchbooks, clay, and other essential tools for our Art and Crafts program.
    • Tie-Dye Kits: Complete kits with dyes, fabric materials, and protective gear for tie-dye projects.
    • Dance Attire: Recommended attire for various dance styles, including leotards, dance shoes, and accessories.

  2. Digital Tools and Software:

    • Computer Labs: Equipped with up-to-date computers and software tailored for learning digital art, basic coding, and creative projects.
    • Online Learning Platforms: Access to educational resources, tutorials, and safe online environments to enhance computer skills.

  3. Learning Guides and Curriculum:

    • Lesson Plans: Structured lesson plans for each program to ensure consistent learning progress.
    • Workbooks and Activity Sheets: Age-appropriate workbooks that reinforce lessons learned in class, available for art, dance, and computer programs.
    • Creative Library: A collection of books, videos, and other media focused on art techniques, dance history, and digital creativity.

  4. Parental Support Resources:

    • Parent Workshops: Regular workshops to help parents support their child's creative development at home.
    • Progress Reports: Detailed progress reports shared with parents to track their child's growth and areas for improvement.
    • Community Forum: An online platform for parents to connect, share experiences, and seek advice from instructors and other families.

  5. Community and Collaboration:

    • Student Showcases: Annual exhibitions and performances where students can display their work to family and friends
    • Collaborative Projects: Group activities that encourage teamwork and social interaction among students.
    • Field Trips: Visits to local art galleries, dance performances, and tech centers to inspire and educate students outside the classroom.
